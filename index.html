ABOUT STRIVE

Tata STRIVE is an initiative of the TCIT, aimed at actively bridging the gap between vocational education and industry needs. In FY 2017-18, Tata STRIVE began working under the aegis of Tata Trusts as a result of the Tata Group’s strategy of ‘Simplification, synergy and scale’. The move recognises the close synergies between Tata STRIVE and the broader community interventions of the Tata Trusts. Tata STRIVE is skilling youth from underprivileged backgrounds through innovations in technology, pedagogy, and methodology.

Our Vision


Gainful livelihood for every aspirant

Our Mission

Skill the disadvantaged for employment, entrepreneurship or community enterprise & Collaborate to raise standards in the ecosystem

The Goal

Impact 1 Million people by 2022; enabling and enhancing livelihoods directly or through eco-system strengthening

Junior Full Stack Java Developer

Eligibility: STEM Graduates (Age Group: 22 TO 27 Years)| Duration: 16 weeks

Course overview

Learn the technical skills, knowledge and competencies necessary to work efficiently as a Junior Full Stack Developer. In this job role, you will often work within a team of developers to create new websites and applications. You may also work in tech-based start-ups, mid-large size software development companies, or consultancy firms, among others.

Content

Technical Modules

  • Introduction to the Job Role
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Web Development Fundamentals (Front-End)
  • JavaScript for Web Development
  • Java Programming Fundamentals
  • Java and SpringBoot
  • Introduction to React
  • Introduction to Relational Databases Software development fundamentals

Non-Technical (Soft Skills) Modules

  • Growth Mind-set
  • Persistence
  • Personal Responsibility
  • Future Orientation
  • Communication
  • Detail Orientation
  • Proactiveness

Business Development Executive (BDE) in Banking, Financial Services & Insurance (BFSI)

Eligibility: Graduate (any discipline) | Duration: 6 weeks

Course overview

Learn about various banking products and policies and acquire skills for selling these products to customers.

Content

  • Introduction to the BFSI sector
  • Orientation to role and responsibility of a BDE
  • Product knowledge
  • Aligning to a structured sales process
  • Best practices in ongoing and post sales service
  • Managing MIS and CRM
  • Youth Development Modules
